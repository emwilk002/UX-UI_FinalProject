VISION AND MISSION

MN Youth in Jazz was created to prvide opportunities for young people to learn about and participate in jazz music. We bring together young musicians from the Twin Cities metro area to create together alongside industry professionals.

Our Commitment to Inclusion

Minnesota Youth Jazz Bands is committed to educating students in a community in which every member’s voice is valued and respected. We believe that music should be available to all students and that diversity is essential to creativity. We’ll work with our students to provide them what they need to succeed.

North Metro Honor Jazz Band

This 20-person honors band is open to all musicians of high school age in the greater Twin Cities area. The instrumentation is that of a traditional jazz big band. Rehearses at Spring Lake High School.

Mid Level Honor Jazz Band

This 22-person honors band is open to all musicians ninth grade or younger in the Twin Cities area. The instrumentation is of a traditional jazz big band. Rehearses at Justice Page Middle School.
